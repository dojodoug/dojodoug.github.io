About Me

In 2015 I came to the overdue conclusion that I needed to seek out a more challenge driven career. It was time to walk away from landscape design / maintenance and to dive headlong into software development. Indeed, it was an extremely stressful transition but I am forever grateful for the opportunity. The overwhelming and yet simple revelation that flourished from this change and continues today, is my desire to create, to learn, and to problem solve. This is a constant motivator that I am proud to employ everyday.

Blank Stares is an educational app that allows students to anonymously give real-time feedback to an instructor based on their comprehension of the instructional material. This feedback then allows the instructor to tailor the best pace for the class. View live on Heroku or view the code on GitHub.
NOTE: This project was rebranded to Blank Stares after the original responymous repo was created.
